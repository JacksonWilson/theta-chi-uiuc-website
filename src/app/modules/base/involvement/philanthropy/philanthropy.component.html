Philanthropy

At Theta Chi, our motto is to always lend a helping hand. Philanthropy is a big part of who we are and we take pride in this aspect of our Fraternity! Not only do we help out various organizations in the community, but we help other Greek organizations across campus by registering teams for their philanthropy events as well.

- Michael Tzeng (Philanthropy Chair) | mhtzeng2@illinois.edu
