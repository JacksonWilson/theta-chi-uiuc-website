Outside of Rho

At Theta Chi, we take academics very seriously as our motto is “Alma Mater First”. We host weekly study sessions all across campus that serve as a quiet place for Brothers to study. We rent rooms out in the Illini Union, Armory, and Ikenberry Commons on a weekly basis. In addition, our work as students has resulted in brothers and alumni within the last 5 years interning, co-oping, and working for 12 Fortune 500 Companies, many well-acclaimed tech companies and firms, and various start-ups.

- Paul Jeon (Scholarship Chair) | mingwan2@illinois.edu
