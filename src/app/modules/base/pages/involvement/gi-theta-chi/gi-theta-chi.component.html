G.I. Theta Chi

G.I. Theta Chi is Theta Chi’s annual event raising money for the USO. The USO, a non-nonprofit organization, offers all Americans a way to say thank you to our troops and their families through delivering highly valued programs, moral-boosting services and engaging entertainment. With 160 USO locations and nearly 9 million visits – today’s USO is always by their side.

G.I. Theta Chi is a competitive event involving Illinois’ entire campus and the Champaign-Urbana community. Throughout the four days each team competes in military inspired competitions. G.I. Theta Chi is an event that will inspire friendly competition while raising money to support our military forces and their families.
