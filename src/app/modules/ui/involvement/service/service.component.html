Community Service

Over the course of the Spring 2016 semester, Rho Chapter managed to log over 640 service hours. This is by far the largest amount ever logged by Rho and is a prime example of how our fraternity stives to help the community and lend an assisting hand.

Our main service event is the Annual Pet A Pup which encourages students and other community members to donate, volunteer, and help with special needs children and their service dogs.
