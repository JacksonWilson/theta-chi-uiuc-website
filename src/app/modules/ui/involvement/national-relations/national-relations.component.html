National Relations

Theta Chi Rho Chapter was established April 8th, 1916. Since then, it has kept close relations with our national headquarters. Over the years, we have received numerous awards and medals for chapter excellence, with 5 Colley Awards and numerous other prestigious Theta Chi honors; however, our greatest accomplishment is our presence. For 100 years now, Rho Chapter has influenced men of all backgrounds, Greek Life, and the entire campus community. It is an honor to be recognized by our nationals as a centennial chapter.
